About Us

LIPS (Languages Interpreting Peoples Services)

LIPS (Languages Interpreting Peoples Services, LLC) was established in 2018, Michigan, USA with the mission of breaking barriers and building bridges among people with limited proficiency language speaking of different cultures. Our determination is to support you communicate across barriers in language and culture among multilingual population.
